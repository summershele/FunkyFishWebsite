At Home Activites

Dry Land Exercises: 5-10 min of stretching, followed by 3 rounds of 25 squats, 20 jump squats, 15 lunges, 10 jumping jacks, and 5 pushups
Best Stretches for Recovery: stand against a wall and make a field goals with your arms, sliding them up and down the wall. Arm circles, toe touches, and knee hugs.
